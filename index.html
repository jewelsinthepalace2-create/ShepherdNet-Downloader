How to Install on Android
Tap Download App above.
Open the file. If asked, tap Settings and allow "Install Unknown Apps".
Tap Install. Open the app and log in.
Important: After logging in, go to the menu and select "Add Leader" to register your Junior Pastors & Shepherds under you.
How to Install on iPhone
Tap Launch Web App above to open.
Tap the Share button at the bottom of Safari.
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen" .
Tap Add. It will look just like a native app!
Desktop Access
Click Open on Computer to launch the system.
Tip: Press Ctrl + D (Windows) or Cmd + D (Mac) to bookmark it for easy access.
